History & Solomonic Dynasty

The Solomonic dynasty claims descent from Menelik I, son of King Solomon of Israel and the Queen of Sheba (Makeda). It ruled Ethiopia from 1270 to 1974 — one of the longest-reigning dynasties in world history. The Ark of the Covenant is believed to be in Axum.

Great Emperors

⚔️ Emperor Menelik II (1889-1913)

Defeated Italy at the Battle of Adwa (1896) — first African victory against European colonization. Founded Addis Ababa and modernized Ethiopia.

👑 Emperor Haile Selassie I (1930-1974)

The 225th Solomonic emperor. Modernized Ethiopia, founded the African Union, and became a global symbol of African independence.

🛡️ Emperor Tewodros II (1855-1868)

'The Unifier' — began modernizing Ethiopia and reunified warring regional lords.

Geography & Nature

Ethiopia is called the 'Roof of Africa' — from Ras Dashen (4,550m, 4th highest in Africa) to Danakil Depression (-125m, one of the hottest places on Earth).

Religion & Faith

Ethiopia is one of the first Christian nations (4th century AD). The Ethiopian Orthodox Tewahedo Church is one of the oldest in the world. Harar is the 4th holiest city in Islam with 82 mosques. The Beta Israel (Ethiopian Jews) are one of the oldest Jewish communities.

Ethiopian Calendar

Current Year: 2017 (7-8 years behind Gregorian calendar) | Months: 13 months (12 months of 30 days + Pagume — 5 or 6 extra days) | New Year (Enkutatash): September 11 (Meskerem 1) | Famous saying: 'Ethiopia has 13 months of sunshine' | Time: Day starts at dawn (6:00 AM = 12:00 Ethiopian time)

Great Victories

⚔️ Battle of Adwa (March 1, 1896)

Emperor Menelik II led Ethiopian forces to defeat 17,000 Italian soldiers. Ethiopia remains the ONLY African nation to never be colonized. Adwa Victory Day is celebrated annually on March 1st.
